About Me

I am a fourth year student at Bucknell University pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering. I like to work close to the hardware side and have dabbled with firmware as a result. Therefore, I am interested in pursuing a career in the field of embedded systems engineering. My work experience with Arduino has resulted in related hobby projects and Raspberry Pi interfacing. Additionally, the various forms of human-computer interaction fascinate me. I would love to help contribute to advances in this sector, and hopefully revolutionize the way people connect with their devices.

Sikorsky

At Sikorsky Aircraft, a Lockheed Martin company, I was tasked with updating the database used in their estimating department. This involved decomposing a collection of Excel sheets into a relational model. I then built an Access based solution from this model. In order to future proof the system, I automated the system of polling an off-site database for up to date resources. To improve the system's ease of use, I added a frontend to the program. This GUI also provided functionality which could automate various tasks which are common in the estimating process.

HydroSense

HydroSense provides a communication protocol and back-end server infrastructure to enable any Arduino or Raspberry Pi to easily collect and publish hydroclimatic data. This data can then be queried, compared, and downloaded by researchers and hobbyists alike.
Keep up to date on the project with our Github page.

Hawkeye

At Hawkeye Technologies, I worked in the assembly and testing of infrared sources and heating elements. This involved tasks such as coil winding, spot welding, and cement casting. Occasionally I would have to machine headers and ceramics. Afterwards, I would perform bulk data collection on the newly finished batch and analyze it for any inconsistencies.
In my last year at Hawkeye, I was tasked with creating datasheets for the entire product catalog. As the company had recently undergone a change in management, I would also be utilizing the new branding. This including prepping the new website and updating schematics from old CAD files.

Solar
