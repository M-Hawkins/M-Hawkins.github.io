About Me

I am a fourth year student at Bucknell University pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering. I like to work close to the hardware side and have dabbled with firmware as a result. Therefore, I am interested in pursuing a career in the field of embedded systems engineering. My work experience with Arduino has resulted in related hobby projects and Raspberry Pi interfacing. Additionally, the various forms of human-computer interaction fascinate me. I would love to help contribute to advances in this sector, and hopefully revolutionize the way people connect with their devices.
