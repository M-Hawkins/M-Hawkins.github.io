HydroSense

HydroSense provides a communication protocol and back-end server infrastructure to enable any Arduino or Raspberry Pi to easily collect and publish hydroclimatic data. This data can then be queried, compared, and downloaded by researchers and hobbyists alike.
Keep up to date on the project with our Github page.

Hawkeye

Occasionally I would have to machine headers and ceramics. Afterwards, I would perform bulk data collection on the newly finished batch and analyze it for any inconsistencies.
In my last year at Hawkeye, I was tasked with creating datasheets for the entire product catalog. As the company had recently undergone a change in management, I would also be utilizing the new branding. This including prepping the new website and assembling a product catalog.
